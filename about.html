About Me

I’m a Bay Area–based CS master’s student who likes turning real needs into products people actually use. I enjoy owning the whole build—from shaping the idea and information flow, to crafting a calm, polished UI, to wiring up the backend, data, and deployment so it feels reliable in the real world.

I care a lot about user preferences and the small details that make an interface feel effortless: what confuses people, what slows them down, and what makes them feel at ease. Right now I’m focused on frontend and full-stack projects, building experiences that look simple on the surface but are thoughtfully engineered underneath.
